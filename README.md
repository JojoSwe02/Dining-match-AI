# Dining-match-AI

## Summary
The purpose of the project is to make decision-making easier, where and what to eat in a restaurants while on a trip or for everyday life. The purpose for the app is to help people to make faster decisions, when hungry and tired. Decisions are harder to make when hungry, and meanwhile lack energy. 

## Background
The problem is that it is hard sometimes to decide what and where you should eat, when out on a trip or in everyday life. From personal experience this is a common problem, and people don't always know what they are craving for dinner after a long day. The purpose for the app is to help people to make faster decisions, when hungry and tired. Decisions are harder to make when hungry, and meanwhile lack energy. 

## How is it used?
The app could be used by many groups of people, but mainly not kids. Young adults and all ages above would be affected by this. One example of use is that you may be in a new town on a trip and don't know what food you want, what you crave or what restaurants are good in that town. The solution to this is to open the app, answer some quick questions and you get recommendations based on your preferences.

## Data sources and AI methods
The app would need different types of data. It needs geographic location, menus, Rating and the AI needs to ask some questions for what preferences the user has. The AI could use recommendations and with algorithms analyze the user preferences. 

The app would also need ratings, so the app doesn't recommend restaurants that are bad food. The recommendations could be from google, foodora or other food delivery sites. The system could use filtering algorithms to reduce the number of restaurant options based on factors such as ratings, cuisine type, distance, and price. Over time, the AI could learn from the user’s choices and make more accurate recommendations.

## Challenges
One challenge is that users may not know what they want to eat, which can make it difficult for the system to provide accurate recommendations. If the user has no clear preferences, the suggestions may not always match what they are looking for. Another challenge is that the preference system requires a large amount of data. 

When users include many preferences, the system must process more information to generate recommendations. While this can lead to more precise suggestions, it may also be difficult if the system does not have enough data available. Therefore, the app would require large and reliable data storage to function effectively.

## What next?
Over time the app will learn the preferences and habits, which allows the AI to give more personalized restaurant recommendations. In the future could the app be connected to the restaurant and allow the users to book tables through the app directly.
